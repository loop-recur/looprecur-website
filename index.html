Who or what is loop/recur?

In more technical terms:
loop/recur's engineers build test-driven mobile back ends and front ends with great attention paid to user experience and design.

In useless buzzwords:
loop/recur collaboratively engineers data-driven mobile and cloud based applications to leverage the web 2.0 aspects of your business with a focus on scalability, best practices, and modularity. It's a win-win.

While our main technology choices are, Javascript, Node.js, Drupal and Objective-C our team has years of experience in multiple languages such as Ruby/Rails, Haskell, Erlang and PHP.

Our Work

  • Netgear NeoTV Remote

    Overview and Functionality:
    The NeoTV Remote was built to control the Netgear NeoTV. Over wifi, the remote auto-connects via Bonjour and controls the onscreen cursor, keyboard, and navigation. The favorites feature allows you to save the channels you watch most from it's built in channel listing and allows you to post what you're watching to Facebook. Other features include vibration and sound feedback control, multiple box support and gesture control of onscreen functions.

    Platforms:
    iPhone and Android

    Technology Used:
    Javascript, Objective-C, Java, Jasmine

  • NJ Property Fax

    Overview and Functionality:
    NJ Property Fax provides a number of different geo based information services to homeowners and real estate agents. Focusing on flood, tidelands, and wetlands maps, users can get information about potential properties they currently own or are interested in purchasing.

    Platforms:
    iPhone and Android

    Technology Used:
    Ruby/Rails, Javascript

  • Webcapsule

    Overview and Functionality:
    Webcapsule is a social story sharing website. Users tell a story about an event or events very important to them, tag people in the story, and share it with those closest to them.

    Platforms:
    iPhone and Android

    Technology Used:
    Ruby/Rails, Javascript, Jasmine, RSpec

  • Webcapsule Mobile

    Overview and Functionality:
    Webcapsule mobile is the companion app to Webcapsule.com. Once you log in via Facebook, Twitter, or Webcapsule.com credentials, the app allows you to record video within a few seconds of entering the application. The app finds your location, attaches a date, allows you to tag friends, add photos and comments to videos and share on Facebook or Twitter. Feeds of your friends' stories, your stories, and your followers/followees are available in app.

    Platforms:
    iPhone and Android

    Technology Used:
    Javascript, Objective-C, Java, Jasmine
